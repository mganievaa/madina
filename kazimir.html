Kazimir Type Specimen

This project is a multi-page specimen book designed to showcase the Kazimir typeface. The aim was to explore the possibilities of editorial design by working with grids, hierarchy, and OpenType features while experimenting with bold typographic compositions. The process involved editing supplied text to suit the design, pairing fonts for balance, and creating layouts that communicate with clarity and purpose. The final outcome highlights Kazimir’s character across different weights and styles, while also reflecting my growing understanding of typography, composition, and multi-page document design in InDesign.
