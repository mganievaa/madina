Lionel's Cafe

Lionel’s Café is a community-focused, budget-friendly coffee shop located on the University of Melbourne campus, known as a creative hangout for VCA students and staff. Rather than functioning like a typical commercial coffee chain, Lionel’s has a relaxed, social atmosphere that encourages connection, conversation, and creativity. This rebranding project explored ways to enhance the café’s existing strengths while updating its identity to better appeal to a younger, student-driven audience. The goal was not to change what makes Lionel’s special, but to highlight its welcoming and creative character through a more modern visual approach. A bubbly typeface was chosen to give the brand a playful and friendly feel, while earthy beige tones were used to create warmth and calm, reinforcing the café’s comfortable, student-centered environment.
