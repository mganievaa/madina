Bahoré

This branding project was created for a community-focused Nowruz Festival in Melbourne — a vibrant, family-friendly event celebrating the Persian, Afghan, and Central Asian New Year. Designed to run the weekend before Nowruz, the festival invites diaspora families to reconnect with their cultural heritage through music, food, dance and intergenerational activities. The brand identity captures the spirit of the occasion: warm, joyful and proud. It speaks directly to second-generation migrants, elders and young families, while remaining inclusive to the wider Melbourne community. The visual direction balances tradition with contemporary energy, honouring deep cultural roots without resorting to over-explaining — a celebration by the community, for the community.
