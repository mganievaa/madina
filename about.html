About

I am a graphic design student working across digital media, with a focus on graphic design, photography, videography, and content creation. I am interested in creating work that emphasizes clarity, structure, and strong visual composition.

My practice is informed by minimalism and a curiosity for cultural references. I enjoy exploring how elements of culture, tradition, and visual heritage can shape and enrich contemporary design, particularly within digital environments.

Alongside my studies, I have gained practical experience across design and media production, which continues to influence my approach toward developing cohesive and adaptable visual outcomes.
